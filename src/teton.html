Grand Teton

The high peaks of Grand Teton National Park will blow you away, with the addtion of the old west charm

About

Grand Teton National Park is a must see. The geography of the park is very interesting, due to the mountain range protruding from a relatively flat valley, it highlights the intensity of this mountain range. Grand Teton has a lot to offer in terms of what there is to see, even if you are just driving through to see the other national parks in the area.

Location

Grand Teton National Park is within the great state of Wyoming, and is sandwiched between the popular town of Jackson Hole, and Yellowstone National Park. If you are staying in Jackson Hole, Grand Teton is a great place to visit if you are on your way to Yellowstone. But do not sleep on this treasure of a national park, you can spend as much time here as you can with Yellowstone.

The Scenic Drive

This park offers a great way to get to Yellowstone. There is so much to see here, and being able to drive through it gives you an opportunity to see two national parks in one day!

Glacier Lakes

Grand Teton offers two glacier lakes to hike around and explore. The water in these lakes is so clear and offers an amazing foreground to the breathtaking peaks above.

Jackson Hole

With Jackson Hole being just south of this park, it offers an awesome place to stay, while you enjoy the things this park has to offer. Experience what it was like to live in the wild west within the town square and the old buildings of this town.
