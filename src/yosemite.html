Yosemite

Experience mother nature's amphitheater firsthand as you traverse this one of a kind national park

About

Yosemite National Park offers an experience like no other. Experience the stunning rock formations that have taken millions of years to form for our beholding. This park hits the even more adventurous side of its visitors offering different opportunities to get up close and personal, whether that is rock climbing or hiking up Half Dome.

Location

Yosemite is located within California’s Sierra Nevada mountains. Take in the beautiful granite cliffs while you travel to your next hike or climb within this national park.

The Cliffs

The granite cliffs are truly something to behold while visiting this park. Weather it is the go to locations of Half Dome, or El Capitan. Yosemite’s beauty will not disappoint.

Waterfalls

In addition to viewing the cliffs, you might stumble across a waterfall here and there. Sunsets might also offer a golden effect on these waterfalls if you are lucky enough to see it.

Take the Scenic Route

Get out the car and enjoy what this park has to offer! Hike amongst the giant ancient sequoia trees!
